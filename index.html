ABOUT

I'm a third year Ph.D. student at Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering, advised by Arvind Krishinamurthy. I received B.S. and M.S. of Computer Engineering at Hanyang Univ. in 2008 and 2010, respectively. I joined Samsung in 2013 and developed high-density microserver architecture for 3 years. Then I moved to S/W Development Team in 2016, to join the research of the most advanced storage device. Before staring my Ph.D. journey, I implemented NVMe-oF systems, PCIe peer-to-peer solutions, and FPGA-based SmartSSD. Now, I'm challenging another big step to prepare for the next decade at UW.
